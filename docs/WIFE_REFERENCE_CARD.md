# Carlos's Sleeve Surgery — Quick Reference for [Wife]

**Surgery: Wednesday June 24, 2026, 9:00 AM**
**Allergies: PEANUT + SHELLFISH (severe — flag on every form)**

---

## 🚨 Call 911 or surgeon IMMEDIATELY if Carlos shows any of these:

- Resting heart rate **>120 bpm** (this is the earliest leak sign)
- Fever **>101°F / 38.3°C**
- Severe / worsening belly pain not helped by meds
- **Left shoulder or chest pain** (referred pain from leak)
- Trouble breathing, fast breathing, blue lips
- Vomiting that won't stop
- Can't keep ANY fluid down for 4+ hours
- Confusion, chills with shaking, very little urine
- Incisions red, oozing, or worse after 48–72 hrs

**Keep his Apple Watch / pulse oximeter on. Check HR several times a day for the first 2 weeks.**

---

## First 3 Days at Home

**What he can have:** water (sips!), broth (NOT seafood broth), sugar-free Jell-O, sugar-free popsicles, decaf tea, Crystal Light, Powerade Zero

**What helps him:**
- Walk with him every 1–2 hrs (prevents clots)
- Make him use the spirometer every hour awake
- Heating pad on shoulders (CO2 gas pain)
- Gas-X strips
- Wedge pillow / recliner — no flat sleeping
- Don't let him drink with a straw or have anything carbonated

**What he CANNOT do:**
- Drive while on narcotic pain meds
- Lift anything >10 lb for 4 weeks
- Lie flat (sleep on wedge/recliner 1–2 weeks)

## Week 1 — Full Liquids (2–4 oz per "meal")

Add to clear liquids:
- Protein shakes (Premier Protein, Fairlife, **Owyn = peanut-free brand**)
- Greek yogurt thinned with milk
- Cream soups (strained, NO chowder/bisque/shellfish)
- Sugar-free pudding
- Blended cottage cheese

**Goal: 60–80g protein/day, 48–64 oz water/day**

---

## The Forever Rules (help him remember)

1. **Protein first** every meal
2. **No drinking 30 min before / 60 min after eating** — biggest rule
3. **Sip slowly, no straws, no carbonation**
4. **Chew 20–30 times per bite**
5. **Stop at first pressure** — not "full"

## NEVER bring home / cook with

- Anything with peanuts or peanut traces (read every label)
- Any shellfish — no shrimp, crab, lobster, scallops, mussels, oysters
- No seafood broth or bisque
- For now (first 6+ months): no rice, no bread, no pasta, no tortillas, no fried food, no sugar, no soda, no alcohol

---

## Cooking together — "Plate Split" technique

When you cook a regular meal:
1. Pull out Carlos's portion FIRST: **3 oz protein + 1–2 Tbsp veggie** (no rice/bread/pasta for him)
2. Plate it on a small (6") salad plate so it doesn't look sad
3. Make your normal plate
4. Eat together — same food, different portions

---

## How you can help most

- Remind him to sip water all day (no straws)
- Remind him: no drinking with meals
- Walk with him daily
- Keep treats out of sight first 6 months
- Be patient with mood swings months 2–4 (hormones releasing from fat)
- Hair loss months 3–6 is normal — it grows back
